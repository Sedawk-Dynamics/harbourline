## Products & Services

### 1. Ship Machinery
Complete range of ship machinery solutions including main engine components, propulsion systems, and vessel machinery parts. We source and supply OEM and aftermarket machinery for all major marine engine brands.

### 2. Main & Auxiliary Engine Spares
Comprehensive inventory of spare parts for main propulsion engines and auxiliary power units. Includes cylinder liners, pistons, piston rings, fuel injectors, turbochargers, bearings, gaskets, and complete overhaul kits.

### 3. Navigation Equipment
Modern navigation instruments and systems including GPS receivers, radar systems, ECDIS (Electronic Chart Display), autopilot systems, compass equipment, AIS transponders, and communication systems.

### 4. Automation Products
Marine automation and control systems including engine monitoring systems, alarm and safety systems, remote control equipment, PLC-based automation solutions, and integrated bridge systems.

### 5. Electrical Machinery
Marine-grade electrical equipment including generators, alternators, switchboards, transformers, electric motors, UPS systems, battery systems, and complete electrical panel solutions.

### 6. Air Compressors
High-pressure and low-pressure marine air compressors for starting air, control air, and service air applications. Includes screw compressors, reciprocating compressors, and associated spare parts.

### 7. Governors
Engine speed governors and control systems for marine diesel engines. Includes electronic governors, hydraulic governors, mechanical governors, and actuator systems from leading manufacturers.

### 8. Air Starters
Pneumatic starting systems for marine diesel engines. Includes air starter motors, vane-type starters, turbine-type starters, and complete starting air systems with associated valves and controls.

### 9. Deck Machinery
Complete range of deck equipment including winches, windlasses, capstans, mooring equipment, hatch covers, davits, and crane systems for cargo handling and vessel operations.

### 10. Anchor Chain & Equipment
Marine anchor chains, anchors, chain cables, swivels, shackles, and associated mooring equipment. Available in various grades (Grade 2, Grade 3) compliant with classification society standards.

### 11. Crane Equipment
Marine cranes and lifting equipment including provision cranes, cargo cranes, hose handling cranes, and associated wire ropes, hooks, and lifting accessories.

### 12. Technical Consulting & Surveys
Professional marine technical consulting services including vessel condition surveys, machinery inspections, pre-purchase surveys, damage assessments, and technical advisory for fleet management.
